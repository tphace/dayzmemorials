The Memorials of DayZ by tphace

Over time, I've gathered as much information as possible about these in-game memorials and the stories behind them. Some remain anonymous, but I've been able to uncover names and backgrounds for a few of the players being honored — and collected everything here on this page.

I hope it helps you plan your own journey through Chernarus or Livonia, so you can visit these places and pay tribute to those we've lost.

If you come across a memorial that's not on the map, feel free to reach out on Reddit (u/tphace) or Discord (tphace#9557). And if you know the story behind a specific cross — especially if it was placed for someone close to you — I'd genuinely like to hear it.

Big thanks to everyone who's contributed so far.
